    ABOUT US

    ﻿﻿San Jose Gun Trader is a full service gun dealer that has been in business since 2012.  We pride ourselves on being the "un-gun store." You won't find any bad attitudes or pushy sellers here.  We are here to help you find the right gun, for the right price, to suit your needs.  We have over 20 years of gun experience including special training and extensive gun knowledge.  We are welcoming to both the gun newbies with lots of questions all the way up to the seasoned gun owner looking to add to their collection.  We sell new and used guns, facilitate transfers, handle private party transfers, purchase used guns, and offer special orders for no additional fees.  Feel free to contact us with any and all questions or requests- we are here to help!    ﻿﻿
