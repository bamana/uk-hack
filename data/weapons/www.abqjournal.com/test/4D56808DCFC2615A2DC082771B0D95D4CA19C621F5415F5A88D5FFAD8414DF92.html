REQUEST FOR PROPOSALS #SIP-004 Fiber Network Infrastructure and Strategic Planning Services 1. INVITATION FOR PROPOSALS The Pueblo de San Ildefonso (the "Pueblo") is issuing a Request for Proposal (RFP) # SIP-004 for qualified and experienced consultants or consulting firm to assist the Pueblo with developing a Fiber Network Infrastructure and Strategic Plan for Services. The Fiber Network Infrastructure and Strategic Plan for Services could serve approximately 2000 residential and community units located within the exterior boundaries of the Pueblo in the 10-year planning horizon. The Fiber Network Infrastructure and Strategic Plan for Services is estimated to include conduits and overhead service lines for fiber. Sealed Proposals: Proposers will deliver one (1) reproducible unbound original and four (4) original copies to the following address: Pueblo de San Ildefonso Administration Building C/O Darren Stand 02 Tunyo Po Santa Fe, NM 87501 SUBMIT PROPOSAL BY: 4:00 PM ON OCTOBER 23, 2015. Proposals received after the above-cited time will be considered late and are not acceptable unless waived by the Tribal Administrator. Emailed proposals are not allowed. The envelope must be clearly marked "SEALED RFP SIP-004, San Ildefonso FIBER NETWORK INFRASTRUCTURE AND STRATEGIC PLAN FOR SERVICES" Please direct any questions regarding this RFP to Darren Stand, Legal Counsel at 505.455.4118, email: dbstand@sanipueblo.org. A Mandatory Pre-Proposal Meeting will be held at the above address 1:30P.M. on October 9, 2015. Journal: October 7, 2015         Published on: Wed October 07, 2015
