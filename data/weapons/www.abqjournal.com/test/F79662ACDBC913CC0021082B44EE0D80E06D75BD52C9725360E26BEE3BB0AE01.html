Red flag raised due to lightning; fireworks still a go

Fireworks go off on the east side of Ballon Fiesta Park as festivities come to an end Saturday evening. (Roberto E. Rosales/Journal)

By Robert Browman / Of the Journal
Published: Saturday, October 11th, 2014 at 3:19pm
Updated: Saturday, October 11th, 2014 at 7:14pm

7:15 p.m.

Rain is now falling on the field at Balloon Fiesta Park.

7:13 p.m.

This evening’s fireworks show is set to start at 7:30 p.m., Balloon Fiesta officials said.

7:09 p.m.

Balloon Fiesta officials are flying the red flag, signaling that balloon events have been canceled due to lightning.

5:15

Officials at Balloon Fiesta Park are flying the green flag, signaling tonight’s events will proceed as scheduled.

3:32

Winds are running 3 mph at Balloon Fiesta Park Saturday afternoon.

Schedule

Evening Session
1:30 p.m. – AIBF Chainsaw Carving Invitational
5:30 p.m. – AIBF Chainsaw Carving Judging, Raffle/Auction
5:45 p.m. – Night Magic™ Glow
8:00 p.m. – AfterGlow™ Fireworks Show*, presented by the Albuquerque Journal and Comcast Spotlight

Traffic

Traffic is expected to be heavy on I-25 northbound and southbound, and on Tramway and Alameda approaching Balloon Fiesta Park. Check our Balloon Fiesta traffic map for current traffic speeds.

Park & Ride is always running. Here are the locations:

East Side

– Hoffmantown Church – 8888 Harper Road NE, Park in the northeast lot only.
– Cliff’s Amusement Park – 4800 Osuna Road NE near San Mateo and I-25.
– Coronado Mall – 6600 Menaul Boulevard NE, park in the southwest corner near San Pedro

West Side

– Intel (southern parking lot) – Highway 528/Rio Rancho Blvd. Enter the Park & Ride lot at either Westside Blvd. or 21st street and follow the signs.
– Lowe’s Lot at Unser Crossing – Southwest corner of Central Avenue SW and Unser Blvd. SW.
