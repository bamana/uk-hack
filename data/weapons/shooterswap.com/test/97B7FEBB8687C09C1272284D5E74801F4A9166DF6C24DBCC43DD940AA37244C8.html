Survive the apocalypse in style with the Conquest Evade

0

admin Gun Blog, Uncategorized August 17, 2012


Evade zombies with this tank-like luxury vehicle. Built on a Ford F550 Super Duty chassis, the Conquest Evade offers a front and rear commercial grade air ride suspension, power windows, and 360-degree roof mounted, joystick-controlled searchlights. EVADE is offered in 4×4, and is available with either a gasoline or diesel engine. EVADE will also come equipped with FLIR night vision camera systems in the front and rear of the vehicle. Don’t forget to stock pile fuel for this bad boy.
