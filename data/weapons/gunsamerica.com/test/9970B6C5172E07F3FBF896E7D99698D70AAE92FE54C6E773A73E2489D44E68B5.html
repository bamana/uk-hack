Grand Jury Indicts Will Hayden for Aggravated, Forcible Rape

by S.H. Blannelberry on December 19, 2014

Related Tags: Buzz

Send to Kindle
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+
  • E-mail
  • Reddit

An East Baton Rouge Parish grand jury indicted ‘Sons of Guns’ television star Will Hayden on three counts of rape Wednesday, the Times-Picayune reported.

Hayden, 49, stands accused of raping two young girls, one of which is believed to be his daughter.

According to the police records, Hayden’s daughter told investigators back in July that she was raped “almost daily,’ since March of 2013 when she was only 11 years old, and that she didn’t say anything because Hayden told her, “don’t tell them nothing because I’m all you got.”

The other alleged victim claims that Hayden raped her in the early 1990s when she was a preteen. She told police that Hayden abused her while she was staying at his home following a fire that burned down her family’s house.

Frank Holthaus, Hayden’s legal representation, did not offer a comment as he had yet to see the indictment.

If convicted on all three counts — two counts of aggravated rape and one count of forcible rape — Hayden would be given a mandatory life sentence.
