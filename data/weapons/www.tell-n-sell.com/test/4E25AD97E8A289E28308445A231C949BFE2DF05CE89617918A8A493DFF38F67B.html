Whether you place your ad via the Internet, phone, mail or FAX, it will appear in the print and Internet editions. The Internet edition currently comes out 4 days after the print edition. Thank you for your ad.

Free ads are for individuals selling something belonging to them. A free ad is not for anyone conducting business. Think of it like the difference between a yard sale and a flea market. A yard sale is free, a flea market isn't. If you are a car dealer with a car or truck for sale, this would be a paid ad. If you are a real estate agent with a house for sale, this would be a paid ad.
