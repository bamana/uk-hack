Job Description

Dynamic, professional and energetic manager for our Salt Lake City, Utah store, able to create, cultivate, enhance business relationships, effectively manage sales personnel and staff. Knowledgeable of sales strategies, capable trainer and motivational leader who can drive production, encourage a competitive and positive work environment and drive company growth. Location: Salt Lake City, Utah Immediate Supervisor: Dan T. B2B & B2C Compensation: We offer competitive base salaries and a rewarding sales incentive compensation plan. $50,000-$70,000 / year Education Required: Bachelor's Degree in a business discipline. Experience in excess of 10 years may substitute for the academic requirement. Personality, attitude and energy is more important than experience. Wage Type: Salary + Commisions Hours: Full time, including some Saturdays. Schedule: During store hours: 8-6 Monday-Friday; 9-4 Saturday; Off Sunday Benefits: Two Weeks PTO / Yr. after trial period, Dental More Details: Work with beautiful Stone, building lasting relationships! American Stone is a local family-owned landscape and building stone retailer. We are a vertically integrated supplier of a wide variety of stone and stone products for landscaping, masonry, construction contractors, and retail DIY customers. We own and operate quarries in multiple locations in Utah, Idaho, Arizona and elsewhere producing sandstone, quartzite, limestone, and granite for a wide variety of applications including walls, walkways, driveways, fences, fire pits, planter boxes, ponds and other water features. Our stone is available in slabs, boulders, flagstone, and crushed selections. Our customers can also buy gravel, sand, and other bulk ground cover products from our yards. Other: Principals only. Recruiters, please don't contact this job poster. Please do not contact job poster about other services, products or commercial interests. Software Knowledge: Basic computer skills required, such as e-mail use, Google Documents, and Microsoft Word. Physical Requirements: Must be able to do occasional physically demanding labor helping to load customers. Pre-employee Screening Requirements: Pre-employment Drug Screening, Online Personality Test, Online Sales Skills Test.

Responsibilities

Duties: Responsible for sales as an individual contributor, as well as territory and staff manager. Plan for 10% travel to visit targeted customers in the area to teach and train sales staff, educate potential customers, and enhance relationships with existing customers. Recruit and manage sales staff and sales support personnel. Develop new business opportunities in collaboration with company marketing, business development personnel and initiatives. Support the development and preparation of quotes and proposals to contractors/masons/installers and service retail DIY customers. Analyze market opportunities, competitive conditions and stone product demand. Forecast sales and gross profit by stone type by month on a rolling 12-month basis. Attend and periodically present at conferences and expositions to support business development activities. Participate in Business Improvement Team Meeting. Lead Company wide "Weekly Sales Meeting" on a monthly basis.

Qualifications

Prior Experience: Recommended 5-10 years experience selling / managing sales. Experience in the landscape products industry with buyers, such as landscapers, construction contractors, masons and DIY home owners a plus. Required Skills: Dynamic, energetic and outgoing personality. High level of organizational skills. High energy, positive, and optimistic attitude. Able to cultivate, maintain, and enhance relationships with all types of customers for the full range of products and solutions offered by the company, including architects, landscapers, construction contractors, and masonry professionals. Capable of creating a competitive and positive working environment that motivates salespeople to accomplish goals. Established local contacts in the landscape, masonry, and construction industries.
