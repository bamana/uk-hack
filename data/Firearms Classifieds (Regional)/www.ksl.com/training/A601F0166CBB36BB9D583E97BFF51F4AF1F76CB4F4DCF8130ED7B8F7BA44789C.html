Job Description

EXPERIENCED CABLE INSTALLERS/TECHNICIANS *If you have over one year experience in triple play cable installations, or satellite installations you are eligible to receive up to $2,500.00 sign on bonus. Duties Include • Installation of cable TV services into residential homes. • Installing overhead drops and outlets to service residential homes • Connecting equipment to customer TV's, Computers ETC • Direct contact with customers in the home, good communication and customer service skills a MUST. • Configuring advanced services to customer’s equipment. • Ability to repetitively lift 70 pounds • Ability to carry a 28' ladder from truck to pole 5-15 times a day • Ability to use a drill for extended periods of time during the day

Responsibilities

We offer: - Company Vehicle -Company Fuel Card - Equipment and Supplies. - Working weekends is a requirement of the job; requests off are honored based on company needs. - Our employees average $600-$1000 Per Week -Medical benefits -Dental benefits -Advancement opportunities -Paid Vacation

Compensation is based on experience.
