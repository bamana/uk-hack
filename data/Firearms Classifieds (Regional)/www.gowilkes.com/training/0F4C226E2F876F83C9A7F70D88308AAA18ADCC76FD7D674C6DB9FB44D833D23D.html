East over North

Central over West
Starmount over Elkin
Ashe over Alleghany


East is the team to watch right now. They have had a dramatic rise and are very hot right now. Adkins is now secure because of this run and can do things. His first test will be against his predecessor. North Wilkes is improved as they are more physical and are out of the flashy 2013 offense which was wrongly suited to the 2014 squad. They could surprise East if East isn't prepped because North has a record of that (3 years ago they played Central tough as **** for 3 quarters.)

However I see the cutting of Boles biting them this Friday as East wins.


Central's a huge ? mark right now. They are 4-0 granted that but they haven't played anyone really yet. The two Iredell schools stink, Surry Central's all over the place, and they barely beat a under-achieving Patton team. West meanwhile has came alive after a bad start. The bye week literally helped them as they have been a completely different team since as they destroyed Atkins quite easily and pulled a huge upset over North Iredell. They won by their desire and refusal to loose more than anything. If they play like that Friday they could shock everyone.

It will be down to if West can do the job on D. Central has far superior skill players while West's much bigger on the line. Good game potential here.


Starmount took a little while to get going but now they are. Forbush's coach getting big headed in the Elkin paper must have done wonders because they got the spark they needed and owned Forbush all over and brought that poise home against Thomasville last week. Elkin's struggling to say the least. They have none of the skill guys left from the last 4 years. Their best returning skill guy (Jamiles Hayes,) transferred to Walkertown, they don't have much besides the FB, TE, and one WR. If Starmount shows up early (which they haven't done the last 2 years,) then it will be a long one for the Elks.


Ashe finally got that win last week against underwhelming 4A Alexander Central who is 1-3. Not a surprise really if they won in week 2 or 3 yeah but I figured they would pull it off. Alleghany's struggling right now. They are young and not so big North Stokes pushed them around all night. I think Sessom's coaching will keep them tough but I don't see them winning vs Ashe. Should be a blowout. Ashe will be better tested the next few weeks. Their D is their weakness and has to get better. They were up 45-14 before Ashe started running to run the clock off which they couldn't do and Alexander came back and made a game out of it. If they do that against Central or East then it could be rough. Ashe's O is still good though but not as good as last year.


The conference is up for grabs really. No one has it in the bag yet. The next few weeks will help. Could see another multi champion year.
