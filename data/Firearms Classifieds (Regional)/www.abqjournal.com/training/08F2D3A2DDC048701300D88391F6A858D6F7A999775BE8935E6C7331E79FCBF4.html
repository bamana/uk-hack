SEVENTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT CHILDREN'S COURT DIVISION COUNTY OF SOCORRO STATE OF NEW MEXICO NO. D-0725 JQ-2008-01 STATE OF NEW MEXICO, ex rel CHILDREN, YOUTH AND FAMILIES DEPARTMENT IN THE MATTER OF I.T., CHILD, AND CONCERNING JUANITA TRUJILLO AND RICHARD THOMAS TRUJILLO, RESPONDENTS. NOTICE OF PENDENCY OF ACTION TO THE ABOVE NAMED RESPONDENT: THOMAS RICHARD TRUJILLO You are hereby notified that an action has been filed against you in the said Court and County by the State of New Mexico in which the State of New Mexico has filed a Motion for Termination of Parental Rights with respect to I.T., male child, born to you on January 5, 2008. You are further notified that unless you file a written response to the Motion by no later than the 28th day of January, 20 09 , in the above court, Children's Court Division, located at the Socorro County Courthouse, Socorro, NM, judgment will be entered against you and your parental rights will be terminated. The name of the State's attorney is Twila A. Hoon, whose office address is 475 Courthouse Road, Los Lunas, NM and the telephone number is (505) 865-4634. WITNESS my hand and Seal of the District Court of the State of New Mexico on December 9, 2008. Virginia Vivian Clerk of the District Court Children's Court Division By: Amy R.
