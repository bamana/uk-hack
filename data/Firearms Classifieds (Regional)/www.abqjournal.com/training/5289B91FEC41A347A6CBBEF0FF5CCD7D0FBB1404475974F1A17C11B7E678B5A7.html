STATE OF NEW MEXICO COUNTY OF BERNALILLO SECOND JUDICIAL DISTRICT COURT No. D-202-CV-2014-00571 BOKF, N.A., successor by merger to BANK OF ALBUQUERQUE, N.A., and d/b/a BANK OF ALBUQUERQUE, Plaintiff, vs. SHERRY L. WILLIAMS, TOM L. GELDER, ZIA HOSPICE, INC., ZIA HOMECARE, LLC, and INTERNAL REVENUE SERVICE, Defendants. NOTICE OF SALE NOTICE IS HEREBY GIVEN that on October 16, 2015, at the hour of 10:00 a.m., the undersigned Special Master will, at the main entrance of the Bernalillo County Courthouse, 400 Lomas NW, Albuquerque, New Mexico, 87103, sell all right, title, and interest of Defendants Sherry L. Williams and Tom L. Gelder in and to the hereinafter-described real estate to the highest bidder for cash. The property to be sold is situated in Bernalillo County, New Mexico, is commonly known as 4811 Hardware NE, Albuquerque, New Mexico, 87109, and has the following legal description: Unit number 4811 E. Ste. 3, Unit number 4811 E. Ste. 4, and Unit numbered 4811 E. Ste. 5 of the HARDWARE BUSINESS PARK CONDOMINIUMS as established by that certain Condominium Declaration for Hardware Business Park Condominiums recorded May 10, 2006, in Book A116, page 8109, as Document No. 2006068313 and First Amendment thereto recorded February 20, 2007, in Book A132, page 7538, as Document No. 2007027591, records of Bernalillo County, New Mexico, together with an undivided interest in the common elements thereto. Said condominium regime being situated on: Tract Three-A (3-A) in Block lettered "G" of Cashway Building Materials, Inc., ALLWOODS SUBDIVISION, an addition to the City of Albuquerque, New Mexico, as the same is shown and designated on the plat thereof, filed in the office of the County Clerk of Bernalillo County, New Mexico, on December 19, 2006, in Plat Book 2006C, Page 383. THE FOREGOING SALE will be made to satisfy a judgment rendered by the above Court in the above-captioned matter cause on August 10, 2015, in the amount of $333,573.02, plus post-judgment interest thereon at the statutory rate of 8.75% per annum ($79.97 per diem) from the date of judgment until paid in full (which will amount to $5,437.96 in accrued interest upon the date of sale), this being an action to foreclose a mortgage on the above-described property. The Plaintiff has the right to bid at such sale and submit its bid verbally or in writing. The Plaintiff may apply all or any part of its judgment to the purchase price in lieu of cash. The sale may be postponed and rescheduled at the discretion of the Special Master. NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN that the real property and improvements concerned with herein will be sold subject to any and all patent reservations, easements, all recorded and unrecorded liens not foreclosed herein, and all recorded and unrecorded special assessments and taxes that may be due. The Special Master disclaims all responsibility for, and the purchaser at the sale takes the property subject to, the valuation of the property, affixture of any mobile or manufactured home to the land, deactivation of title to a mobile or manufactured home on the property, if any, environmental contamination on the property, if any, and zoning violations concerning the property, if any. NOTICE IS FURTHER GIVEN that the purchaser at such sale shall take title to the above-described real property subject to a one (1) month right of redemption. Respectfully submitted, /s/Bradford H.
