EIGHTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT IN THE DISTRICT COURT OF COLFAX COUNTY, NEW MEXICO No. D-809-CV-2015-00128 PUEBLO OF SANDIA, a federally recognized Indian tribe, Plaintiff, vs. 1)THE UNKNOWN HEIRS OF THE FOLLOWING NAMED DECEASED PERSONS: CHARLES F. WHITAKER; LUCIOUS B. BLAXLEY;ELIZABETH B. BLAXLEY; RUFUS S. GREEN; ANNA B. GREEN; PAUL PHILLIP; 2)THE AMERICAN AND EUROPEAN INVESTORS ASSOCIATION, INC.; 3)ALL UNKNOWN CLAIMANTS OF INTEREST TO THE PREMISES ADVERSE TO PLAINTIFF, Defendants. NOTICE OF PENDENCY OF SUIT THE STATE OF NEW MEXICO: To the following named defendants against whom constructive service is sought to be obtained, to-wit: 1)THE UNKNOWN HEIRS OF THE FOLLOWING NAMED DECEASED PERSONS: CHARLES F. WHITAKER; LUCIOUS B. BLAXLEY; ELIZABETH B. BLAXLEY; RUFUS S. GREEN; ANNA B. GREEN; PAUL PHILLIP; 2)THE AMERICAN AND EUROPEAN INVESTORS ASSOCIATION, INC.; 3)ALL UNKNOWN CLAIMANTS OF INTEREST TO THE PREMISES ADVERSE TO PLAINTIFF, You, the above-named defendants and each of you, are notified that Pueblo of Sandia, as Plaintiff, has commenced suit against you, and each of you, as defendants in Cause No. D-809-CV-2015-00128, now pending in the District Court of the Eighth Judicial District Court of the State of New Mexico, sitting within and for the County of Colfax, by filing therein its verified Complaint; that the general object of said suit is to determine and quiet the fee simple title of the plaintiff against you, the defendants, and each of you, in and to the following described property, to-wit: That portion of that certain mining claim or extension known as Bolter No. 159 MX which lies West of the boundary line common to and separating the properties of Plaintiff and the Boy Scouts of America (Philmont Scout Ranch). Said claim is located in Section 26, Township 28 North, Range 16 East, New Mexico Principal Meridian, and lies within the boundaries of Plaintiff's lands as shown on Plat No. 07097, Document No. 200703059 recorded July 20, 2007 in the Colfax County Clerk's Office (the "Survey Plat"). together with all and singular the hereditaments and appurtenances thereunto belonging or in anywise appertaining, and the reversion and reversions, remainder and remainders, rents, issues and profits thereof; that unless you, the said defendants and each of you file a responsive pleading or motion on or before the 28th day of October, 2015, judgment as prayed for in the Plaintiff's Complaint will be rendered in said cause by default against you and each of you so failing to appear. The attorney for the plaintiff is Kamm & McConnell, L.L.C., Terrence R. Kamm, P. O. Box 1148, Raton, New Mexico 87740. Dated at Raton, New Mexico this 1st day of September, 2015. BERNABE P.
