STATE OF NEW MEXICO COUNTY OF SANDOVAL THIRTEENTH JUDICIAL DISTRICT No. D-1329-CV-2014-00664 BOKF, N.A., a national banking association dba BANK OF OKLAHOMA as successor by merger to Bank of Albuquerque, N.A., Plaintiff, vs. JACOB D. CARDONA and LAURA J. CARDONA, Defendants. NOTICE OF SALE ON FORECLOSURE PLEASE TAKE NOTICE that the above-entitled Court, having appointed me or my designee as Special Master in this matter with the power to sell, has ordered me to sell the real property (the "Property") situated in Sandoval County, New Mexico, commonly known as 217 Southern Blvd. SW, Rio Rancho, New Mexico 87124, and more particularly described as follows: LOT NUMBERED THIRTY-TWO (32), BLOCK NUMBERED ONE (1), UNIT NUMBERED (9), RIO RANCHO ESTATES, AS THE SAME IS SHOWN AND DESIGNATED ON PLAT ENTITLED, "N.E. PORTION OF UNIT NINE, RIO RANCHO ESTATES, TOWN OF ALAMEDA GRANT, SANDOVAL COUNTY, NEW MEXICO", FILED IN THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY CLERK, SANDOVAL COUNTY, NEW MEXICO, ON JANUARY 31, 1962, IN RIO RANCHO ESTATES PLAT BOOK 1, PAGE 10. The sale is to begin at 2:00 p.m. on October 20, 2015, outside the front entrance of the Sandoval County Courthouse, 1500 Idalia Rd, Bldg A, Bernalillo, New Mexico, at which time I will sell to the highest and best bidder for cash in lawful currency of the United States of America, the Property to pay expenses of sale, and to satisfy the Judgment granted BOKF, N.A.. BOKF, N.A. was awarded in rem Judgment on March 1, 2015, in the principal sum of $154,938.10, plus interest due on the Note through March 1, 2015, in the amount of $14,105.90, and accruing thereafter at the rate of 4.750% per annum ($20.16 per diem) until paid, plus escrow advances for property taxes in the amount of $3,023.63, plus hazard insurance in the amount of $1278.04, plus FHA/HUD premiums in the amount of $2,242.06, plus reasonable attorney's fees incurred by Plaintiff through February 28, 2015, in the amount of $950.00 and costs through February 28, 2015, in the amount of $590.77, with interest on the aforesaid amounts at the rate of 4.750% per annum from date of the entry of this Judgment until paid. The sale is subject to rights and easements of record, to unpaid property taxes and assessments, and to the one (1) month right of redemption in favor of the Defendants as specified in the Judgment filed herein. PROSPECTIVE PURCHASERS AT THE SALE ARE ADVISED TO MAKE THEIR OWN EXAMINATION OF TITLE AND THE CONDITION OF THE PROPERTY AND TO CONSULT THEIR OWN ATTORNEY BEFORE BIDDING. /s/ David Mitchell Special Master c/o 5120 San Francisco Road NE Albuquerque, New Mexico 87109 (505) 858-3303 Journal: September 26, October 3, 10, 17, 2015         Published on: Sat September 26, 2015
