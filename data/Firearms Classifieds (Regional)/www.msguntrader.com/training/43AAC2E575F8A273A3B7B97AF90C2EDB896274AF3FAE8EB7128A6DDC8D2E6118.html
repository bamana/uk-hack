About Us

Welcome to MS Gun Trader, Mississippi’s largest gun classifieds website.  This site’s purpose is to provide a place for Mississippi’s citizens to buy, sell, and barter pretty much anything gun related.  We strongly support the civil liberties that our founding fathers bestowed upon us, and we hope that our site makes it easier for you to express those liberties.

Thanks for supporting our site!

-MS Gun Trader Staff
