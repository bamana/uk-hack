(Missoula) Spanish Revolver in .32-20 – $ 180

Posted on 08/16/2015 by Armslist - Montana Gun Classifieds

For sale is a Spanish copy of S&W Model 1905 revolvers, produced in Eibar, Spain, in 1924. Beneath the left wooden grip panel is “F” and “A” etched into the frame, suggesting this is a “Francisco Arizmendi” manufacture revolver. The caliber is one popular for the time, but now since relegated to antiques or niche shooters. I am not totally sure of the proof marks, but they seem commensurate with manufacturer, location and time period.

The gun itself is in good working condition and was in the possession of a friend before being passed to me as payment of a debt. I had the gun inspected by Chris Barnard of Accu-Arms for mechanical issues and to determine if it was safe to shoot, as well as the internals cleaned and worked over.

Cylinder lockup is solid, and the release mechanism near the forcing cone works perfectly fine. The trigger pull is smooth in both double and single actions, though a strong finger is needed I might think. The barrel has some pitting, but the rifling looks to be in good shape overall. The finish is as is in the photos.

With this gun comes a box of 50 .32-20 as pictured.
