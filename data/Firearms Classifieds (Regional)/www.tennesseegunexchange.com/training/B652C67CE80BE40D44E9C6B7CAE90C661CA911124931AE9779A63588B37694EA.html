3 Self Defense Products No Home Should Have

happygunowner | Home Defense, Safety | August 2, 2014

We shared info, in an earlier post about home defense, about products you NEED in your home.  We also talked a bit about the difference in home and self defense and how the products for each will differ.  Now, let’s look a little closer at self defense items that could be harmful during a home invasion.  While the items listed below are GREAT for self defense, they could pose problems when used to protect your home.  These three self defense items are great for when you’re traveling, going for a jog or any time you are not at home.  Keep at least one of them on your person at all times.

3 Self Defense Products No Home Should Have

  1. Pepper Spray or Mace
  2. Personal Tasers or Stun Guns
  3. Self Defense Batons

Again, let me reiterate that these are GREAT self defense products that I recommend for anyone, when not at home.  In the home, however, they could cause more harm than good.

Teach your children these steps to follow if they encounter a gun

happygunowner | Around the Web, Safety | May 24, 2013

Teach your children these steps to follow if they encounter a gun:

  1. Stop!

  2. Don’t touch.

  3. Leave the area.

  4. Tell an adult.

 

If You Do Not Own A Gun:

  • Talk with your children about the risk of gun injury outside the home in places where they may visit and play.
  • Tell your children to stay away from guns when they are in the homes of their friends.
  • Speak with the parents of your children’s friend to determine whether they have a gun in the home.
  • If they do, urge them to empty it out and lock it up.

 

From the www.tn.gov website. To read more please visit the website here: www.tn.gov/safety/pubsafety/handgunsafety.shtml
