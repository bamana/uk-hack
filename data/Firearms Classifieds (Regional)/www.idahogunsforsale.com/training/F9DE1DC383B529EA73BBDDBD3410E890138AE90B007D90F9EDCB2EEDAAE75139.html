About Us

Lawford Media was founded in 1999 and launched with two very successful online enthusiast publications.   Hotrodhotline.com and BikerHotline.com grew to become the largest and most successful online publications in their niche groups.

In 2012 both of these publications were sold to Internet Brands, leaving us with the opportunity to work on a venture that we had been planning for many years.

Whenever we wanted to sell something, we found that since the local newspapers classifieds were no longer utilized, the available online products left much to be desired.  The anonymity meant that we had no way of knowing who was responding to our ad ...... we dealt with spam and they wouldn't allow the sale of firearms online. 
 
So many negatives .... yet there were just not many options.  We wished for the days of LOCAL advertising where the person placing an ad was identifiable ... where if we needed help placing an ad we could pick up the phone and get a real live person here in Idaho to help.  Where we could actually stop by an office and see who was running the show!
 
We have worked to bring all of the positive aspects of local advertising, combine them with the good parts of online advertising, to give our fellow Idahoans the best possible online selling experience.
